DICE ROLLER

Instructions

Build a list of options using the form on the right. Edit the list (shown in the middle) as necessary.

When your list is built, click "roll dice"! The number & type of dice will automatically be selected based on the number of list items.

NOTE! In order to ensure that all options are possible when 2 or more dice are rolled, a secondary step has been introduced -- instead of always *adding* the results, each new die rolled may be *subtracted* from the running total. One (1) is the lowest you can get, though.
